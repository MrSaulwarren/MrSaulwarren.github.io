Skydiving is both life changing & awesome!

I had always wanted to experience sky diving, but I was always afraid to. I had decided to overcome the fear and commit myself to a full accelerated free fall course at brakely in northamptonshire.

After passing levels 1-8 with flying colours (giggles) and 4 consolidation jumps I had never felt more alive or proud of myself. In total I had successfully jumped 13 times out of a plane and felt amazing for it. I realised once I decide on something and commit to it, I always succeed and I have no doubts in myself that it shall be the same with-in the world of web development / programming too.
