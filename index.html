Entering the world of Web development

Coding in itself forces me to think and learn in new ways i'm not used to, but I welcome and enjoy the challenge of it and wonder where it's going to take me. I find that coding helps me to grow personally and that to me is important as I value the idea of being able to show up for work and be myself.


I want to be able to go into work with a sense of purpose and look forward to my work life instead of drag ones self out of bed in the morning for a means of income just to pay the bills, life is too short for that. coding allows me to build something that doesn't exist yet too and that is just awesome. I come from an artistic background yet I had no idea that in code there is actually an art form to the thought process, layering, structuring, building, styling and organizing the look of what you've built when it comes to a website. I'm learning that web development is a digital statement for both the client and user that it should promote the purpose of a site, look fun, be engaging, clean, functional and simple to use, perhaps even educate and inspire it's users too, that's the kind of programmer I aspire to be as a web developer.
